### 1. Speed Match
**Description:** Race against the clock to select the right answer.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - A 10-second timer counts down for each question.
    - If the timer hits zero, the question is marked incorrect (Timeout).
- **Controls:** Click/Tap the correct option button.
- **Visuals:** A red progress bar indicates remaining time. Options highlight Green (Correct) or Red (Wrong) upon selection.

### 2. Mastery Flashcards
**Description:** Flip cards to peek, then tick the correct answer.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - 4 cards are presented face-down. 
    - Clicking a card flips it to reveal the option text.
    - Users can peek at multiple cards before deciding.
- **Controls:** 
    - **Click Card:** Flip to reveal/hide.
    - **Click 'Select' Tick:** Confirm this card as the answer.
- **Visuals:** 3D card flip animation. Detailed card back design.

### 3. Bubble Pop
**Description:** Catch the floating answers before they disappear.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - Options appear as bubbles floating from the bottom of the screen to the top.
    - Bubbles have different speeds and start times.
    - The question is pinned at the top of the screen.
- **Controls:** Click a bubble to "pop" it and select that answer.
- **Visuals:** Floating animation with sway. Bubbles disappear/pop on interaction.

### 4. Sentence Builder
**Description:** Drag and drop words to complete the sentence.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - A sentence with a blank space (`___`) or a drop zone is displayed.
    - Options are tiled at the bottom.
    - Users drag the correct word into the blank space.
- **Controls:** Drag and drop (or Click) an option to fill the blank.
- **Visuals:** Dashed border drop zone. Tiles snap into place.

### 5. Swipe Decisions
**Description:** Quickly swipe right for correct, left for incorrect.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - Options are presented one by one as a stack of cards (Tinder-style).
    - **Goal:** Swipe **Right** on the *Correct* answer. Swipe **Left** to discard *Wrong* answers.
    - Discarding the correct answer counts as a mistake.
- **Controls:** 
    - Mouse Drag/Swipe or Button click (Cross/Check).
- **Visuals:** Card stack animation. Swiping feedback (Green/Red tints).

### 6. Quiz Master
**Description:** High stakes trivia with lifelines.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - Modeled after "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire".
    - **Lifeline:** "50:50" removes two incorrect options, leaving the correct answer and one distractor.
- **Controls:** Click the answer option. Click the "50:50" button to use the lifeline.
- **Visuals:** Dark blue/black specific TV show aesthetic. Large lettered options (A, B, C, D).

### 7. Mind Match
**Description:** Memory game: Pair questions with their answers.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - Grid of 12 cards (6 pairs of Question + Answer).
    - All cards start face down.
    - Flip two cards; if they match (Question matches its Answer), they stay revealed. If not, they flip back.
- **Controls:** Click cards to flip.
- **Scoring:** +50 points for a match, -5 points for a mismatch.
- **Visuals:** Card grid. Icons distinguish 'Question' cards from 'Answer' cards.

### 8. Space Mission
**Description:** Shoot the asteroids that contain the right answer.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - Options fall from the top of the screen as "asteroids" or enemies.
    - The player controls a ship at the bottom (visually).
    - Clicking an enemy destroys it.
- **Controls:** Click the target enemy.
- **Visuals:** Space background with stars. falling elements. Pulse effects.

### 9. Canyon Jump
**Description:** Jump to the correct stone to cross the wide canyon.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - A character stands at the edge of a cliff.
    - Stones with options appear in the gap.
    - Selecting a stone makes the character jump to it.
- **Controls:** Click the correct stone.
- **Visuals:** 3D perspective of a canyon. Jump animation (scale/translate).

### 10. Tower Builder
**Description:** Stack blocks higher by choosing the right answers.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - A tower grows on the left side of the screen.
    - Correct answers add a new block to the stack.
    - Height represents progress/score.
- **Controls:** Select the correct block option from the right panel.
- **Visuals:** Physics-like block stacking animation. Sky background.

### 11. Orbit Selector
**Description:** Catch the correct option as it orbits the sun.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - The Question is the "Sun" in the center.
    - Options orbit around the center at different distances and speeds.
- **Controls:** Click the moving orbiting planet/option.
- **Visuals:** Circular motion paths. Space/Solar system theme.

### 12. Lane Racer
**Description:** Switch lanes to collect the right answer.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - A 4-lane road with options appearing at the horizon.
    - Player selects a lane to align their car.
    - "Hit Gas" comfirms the selection.
- **Controls:** 
    - Buttons (1-4) or Click Lane to switch.
    - "HIT GAS" button to submit.
- **Visuals:** 3D road perspective. Car moves sideways to lanes.

### 13. Safe Cracker
**Description:** Turn the dial to crack the code.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - A safe dial in the center.
    - Options are positioned at 12, 3, 6, and 9 o'clock.
    - Selecting an option rotates the dial to point to it.
- **Controls:** Click the option to rotate the dial.
- **Visuals:** Rotating knob animation. Metallic/Dark safe aesthetic.

### 14. Treasure Map
**Description:** Dig for treasure, avoid the traps.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - Dig sites marked with 'X' on a map.
    - Clicking a site reveals what's underneath.
    - **Correct:** Reveals a Gem.
    - **Incorrect:** Reveals a Skull.
- **Controls:** Click the 'X' mark.
- **Visuals:** Parchment paper texture. Digging/Reveal animation.

### 15. Potion Lab
**Description:** Brew the correct ingredient elixir.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - Ingredients (options) sit on a shelf.
    - Selecting one "pours" it into the cauldron.
    - **Correct:** Magical sparkle effect.
    - **Incorrect:** Puff of smoke.
- **Controls:** Click the ingredient bottle.
- **Visuals:** Liquid bubbling in cauldron. Flask pouring animation.

### 16. Code Rain
**Description:** Hack the matrix by catching the right code.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - "Matrix-style" green characters rain down.
    - Some streams contain the answer options.
    - Clicking the correct falling text packet hacks the system.
- **Controls:** Click the falling text box.
- **Visuals:** Black background, green monospace text. Binary/Katakana rain effect.

### 17. Penalty Kick
**Description:** Score a goal by aiming at the right target.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - A soccer goal with a goalie.
    - Options are placed in corners/sides of the goal.
    - Player shoots at the chosen target.
    - Goalie dives (randomly or scripted) to try and block.
- **Controls:** Click the target area in the goal.
- **Visuals:** Ball kick animation. Goalie dive animation. Stadium crowd background.

### 18. Train Switch
**Description:** Guide the train to the correct station.
- **Mechanics:** 
    - A train track splits into multiple branches.
    - Each branch leads to a Station (Option).
    - Player switches the track (selects the station) for the train to follow.
- **Controls:** Click the station/track end.
- **Visuals:** Tracks diverging. Train moving along the chosen path.
